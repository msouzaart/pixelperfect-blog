Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 28, 2025

Welcome to our website ("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in compliance with the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and other applicable Canadian privacy laws. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our website and subscribe to our newsletter.

1. What Information We Collect

We collect the following personal information when you sign up for our newsletter:

  • Email Address: We collect your email address to send you weekly updates on the newest design stories, case studies, and tips.

We do not collect any additional personal information unless you voluntarily provide it (e.g., by contacting us directly).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your email address for the following purposes:

  • To send you our weekly newsletter with design stories, case studies, and tips.
  • To manage your subscription, including sending confirmation emails or updates about your subscription status.
  • To respond to any inquiries you may have about the newsletter or our services.

We will not use your information for any purpose other than those stated above without obtaining your consent.

3. How We Share Your Information

We use Kit (formerly ConvertKit) to manage our newsletter subscriptions. Your email address will be shared with Kit for the sole purpose of sending newsletters and managing subscriptions. Kit is required to protect your data in a manner consistent with this Privacy Policy.

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with any other third parties, except:

  • If required by law, such as to comply with a legal obligation, court order, or regulation.
  • With service providers acting on our behalf who are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only for the purposes we specify.

Your data may be processed or stored outside of Canada by Kit. In such cases, it may be subject to the laws of the jurisdiction where it is processed, which may differ from Canadian laws.

4. How We Protect Your Information

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. These measures include using secure third-party services (like Kit) that comply with industry standards for data protection.

We retain your email address only for as long as you remain subscribed to our newsletter. If you unsubscribe, we will delete your email address from our records within 30 days, unless we are required to retain it for legal purposes.

5. Your Rights and Choices

Under PIPEDA, you have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can request to see the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: You can request to correct any inaccurate or outdated information.
  • Unsubscribe: You can unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in any newsletter email. We will promptly remove your email address from our mailing list.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information in the "Contact Us" section below.

6. Consent

By providing your email address and subscribing to our newsletter, you consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of your personal information as described in this Privacy Policy. You may withdraw your consent at any time by unsubscribing from the newsletter, subject to legal or contractual restrictions.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the updated policy on our website with a new "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
